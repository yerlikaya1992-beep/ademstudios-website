Privacy Policy

for the app Bloom (package com.ademstudios.bloom) · Provider: Adem Studios

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains, in line with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), which personal data is processed in the app "Bloom", for which purposes, and what rights you have.

2. Principle

Bloom is a casual merge game that can be played offline. Most of your data — in particular your game progress and settings — is stored locally on your device only and is not transmitted to us. Personal data is processed only to the extent described below through embedded Google services.

3. Data stored locally on your device

Game progress, stars earned, high scores, language and sound/vibration settings, and the "Bloom Pro" status are stored locally. This data leaves your device only if you use cloud saving via Google Play Games (see section 4). Uninstalling the app deletes the local data.

5. Consent & withdrawal (ads / analytics)

Where legally required (in particular in the EEA), your consent is obtained via the Google User Messaging Platform before personalized ads or analytics processing. You can withdraw consent at any time with future effect:

  • in the app under Settings → "Withdraw consent" (shown where required for your region), or
  • by purchasing "Bloom Pro", which permanently disables advertising.

6. Recipients & international transfers

The recipient of the data in section 4 is Google. Data may be transferred to servers in the USA. Google LLC is certified under the EU-US Data Privacy Framework; EU Standard Contractual Clauses serve as an additional safeguard (Art. 46 GDPR).

7. Retention

Local data remains until you uninstall the app or reset it within the app. Retention of data processed by Google follows their privacy terms. Crash and analytics data are kept in pseudonymized form only as long as necessary.

8. Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right of access (Art. 15), rectification (Art. 16), erasure (Art. 17), restriction (Art. 18), data portability (Art. 20) and objection (Art. 21). You may withdraw consent at any time (section 5). You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority. To exercise your rights, contact the e-mail address above.

9. Children

Bloom is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children without parental consent.
